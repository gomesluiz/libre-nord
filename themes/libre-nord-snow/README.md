# Nord Snow Storm Theme

[⬅ Back to main repository](../../README.md)

**Snow Storm** is the light variant of the collection. It uses the bright and icy tones of the palette (`nord4` to `nord6`) to create a clean, high-contrast interface. It is the ideal choice for brightly lit workspaces or for users who prefer the traditional aesthetics of word processors, but with reduced brightness to minimize eye fatigue.
